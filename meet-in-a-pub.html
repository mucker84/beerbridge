Meet in a pub

Every BeerBridge meeting happens in a real Prague pub. No video calls, no shared screens, no classroom vibe — just two people (or a small group) sitting across from each other with a beer.

The pub is a deliberate choice. It's neutral ground. Neither person is the host. The atmosphere is informal by default, which makes it easier to ask questions you'd feel awkward asking in a more structured setting.

We look for places where you can actually have a conversation — not loud music bars or tourist traps. Accessible by public transport, somewhere you'd go anyway.

What to expect:

  • One-on-one or small group (2–4 people)
  • Roughly an hour, no strict agenda
  • You bring your questions, they bring theirs
  • No preparation needed — just show up

First events are planned for spring/summer 2025. We're currently collecting interest — if you'd like to be one of the first, sign up on the main page.
