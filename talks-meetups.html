Talks & meetups

The format is simple: one person talks for about 20 minutes, then everyone discusses it over a beer. No slides required, no expert credentials needed — just something you know and the group doesn't.

We alternate. One event a Czech local talks about something from Czech life — culture, history, how a system works. The next event an expat talks about their country, their experience, their perspective. Everyone learns something in each direction.

Topics we'd like to cover:

  • Czech bureaucracy explained — what you actually need to know as an expat
  • What surprised me most about Prague — an outsider's honest take
  • How Czech culture actually works — informality, pivo, chalupa, the stuff no guide mentions
  • Your country's weirdest rule — everyone brings one, everyone leaves confused

We're still in the planning stage and looking for our first speakers. If you have a topic you'd want to present — or just a topic you'd want someone to cover — let us know.
